Why Sapiens?

  • There are lots of other books.
  • I believe Harari's doesn't know that the field of Sociology exists.
  • Additionally, I don't think Harari understands what academia is in general.
  • If you have even a shallow knowledge of any of the subjects he covers, it will be obvious he has no idea what he's talking about.
  • There are so many good and cool books out there, please go read one of those.
